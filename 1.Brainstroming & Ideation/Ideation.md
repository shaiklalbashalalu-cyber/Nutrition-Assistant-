# Phase 1: Brainstorming & Ideation 💡

This folder contains the initial concepts, problem statements, and brainstorming artifacts for the Nutrition Assistant project.

## Overview
The goal of this phase was to identify the core problem we are solving: helping users easily track and manage their nutritional intake. 

## Contents
* **Problem Statement:** What dietary or tracking challenges are users facing?
* **Target Audience:** Who is this app for? (e.g., fitness enthusiasts, people with dietary restrictions, general health-conscious individuals).
* **Initial Feature Ideas:** 
  * Calorie tracking
  * Macro-nutrient breakdowns
  * Food database search
* **Competitor Analysis:** What existing apps do well and where they fall short.
